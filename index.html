Life of a Gardener

Unfortunately, the way that people think about themselves tends to make us smaller than we really are. We do not see ourselves as what we really are and I wish that we did. I've always wanted to be something that I am not but it is not the way that the world works. Oftentimes people get lost in themselves, imagining that they are somthing else, when, like a plant, the greatest way to grow is to accept our current position and move through it.


To grow our plants, we have to trust that they'll tell us when they're hungry why can't we trust ourselves to do the same thing.
